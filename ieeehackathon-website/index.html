ABOUT US

Prismatrix, the hackathon hosted by IEEE UMIT at the Usha Mittal Institute of Technology, stands as a vibrant celebration of innovation and collaboration. Drawing inspiration from the powerful symbolism inherent in its name, Prismatrix mirrors the way a prism refracts light into a spectrum of colors.
This hackathon provides a unique stage exclusively crafted for UMIT students, offering them an opportunity to apply their skills, gain insights from industry experts, and forge connections with fellow enthusiasts.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Prismatrix?

Prismatrix is a coding competition that explores the Code Spectrum and offers a platform for participants to showcase their coding skills.

How can I register for Prismatrix?

To register for Prismatrix, click on the "Register Using Devfolio" button on the home page and follow the registration process on Devfolio.

What is in for me?

Learning new skills, looks good on your CV, networking, winning cash prizes, working as a team, meet great people, have great food and having fun!

What is the Program/Timetable?

Please check the timeline on the website, small changes might occur.

How many people should a Team be?

A team should be between 2-4 people, if you don’t have a team yet you can still apply because you will have the chance to join a team onsight.

What are the costs of participating?

FREE. Free for spectators. Free for participants.

WILL THERE BE PRIZES?

Absolutely. Check out our prize page for details.

I have a question or concern what should I do?

Contact us. We are here to help and create a great atmosphere.
